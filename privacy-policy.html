Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how SnapCalc collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you visit or interact with this website. The goal is to be clear about what information is involved, why it is used, and what choices are available to you.

SnapCalc is designed to work without accounts or logins. Most visitors can use calculators, utilities, and informational pages without submitting personal details. By using this site, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information we collect

SnapCalc does not collect personal information by default. You are not required to register or provide identifying details to use the tools on this site.

Personal information is collected only when you choose to provide it, such as when submitting a message through the contact form. This may include your email address and the contents of your message, and is used solely to respond to your inquiry.

Certain non-personal information may be collected automatically through standard web technologies. This can include browser type, operating system, device category, approximate location, IP address, pages visited, referral sources, and interaction patterns. This data does not directly identify you.

Cookies and similar technologies

SnapCalc may use cookies and similar technologies to support core site functionality, measure usage patterns, and display advertising. Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help the site understand how it is used.

Third-party services, including analytics providers and advertising networks such as Google AdSense, may also use cookies or similar identifiers. These are governed by the privacy policies of those third parties.

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect how certain features function.

How information is used

Information collected by SnapCalc is used only for purposes related to operating and improving the site. Typical uses include:

  • Maintaining and improving calculator accuracy and usability
  • Understanding how visitors interact with site content
  • Diagnosing technical issues and monitoring performance
  • Responding to messages, feedback, or support requests
  • Displaying advertising and affiliate content where applicable

Collected information is not used for profiling individuals or automated decision-making unrelated to site operation.

Advertising and affiliate disclosure

SnapCalc may display advertising through third-party networks and include affiliate links to external products or services. These third parties may use cookies or similar technologies to measure ad performance and display relevant content.

SnapCalc does not receive access to personal profiles created by advertisers. Any data collected by advertising partners is governed by their own privacy policies.

Data sharing and third parties

SnapCalc does not sell, rent, or trade personal information. Information may be shared only with service providers that support site operation, such as hosting, analytics, or advertising services, and only as needed for those services to function.

Information may also be disclosed if required by law or necessary to protect the rights, safety, or integrity of the site and its users.

Data retention

Personal information submitted through the contact form is retained only as long as necessary to address the inquiry or maintain a reasonable communication record. Non-personal analytics data is retained according to the policies of the relevant service providers.

International use

SnapCalc is accessible worldwide. Depending on your location, information may be processed or stored in countries where service providers operate. By using this site, you consent to such processing where permitted by law.

Your rights and choices

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights related to personal data, such as requesting access, correction, or deletion of information you have provided. Requests can be submitted through the contact page and may require verification.

Children’s information

SnapCalc is not intended for children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so it can be removed.

Policy updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect changes in legal requirements, site functionality, or business practices. Updates will be posted on this page, and continued use of the site indicates acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact information

For questions or concerns related to this Privacy Policy or data practices, please use the contact page. Privacy-related inquiries are reviewed and handled with care.
