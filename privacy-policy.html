Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains in detail how SnapCalc collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you visit or interact with this website. The purpose of this policy is to provide clear, transparent information about data handling practices so that users can understand what information is involved, why it is used, and what choices are available.

SnapCalc is designed to be usable without creating an account or providing personal details. Most visitors can access calculators, utilities, and informational pages without submitting any identifying information. By using this website, you acknowledge and agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

SnapCalc does not collect personal information by default. You are not required to register, log in, or provide identifying details in order to use the calculators and tools available on this site.

Personal information is collected only when you voluntarily choose to provide it. This typically occurs when you submit a message through the contact form. In those cases, the information you provide may include your email address and the contents of your message. This information is used solely for the purpose of responding to your inquiry.

In addition to voluntary submissions, certain non-personal information may be collected automatically through standard web technologies. This can include your browser type, operating system, device category, approximate geographic location, IP address, pages visited, referral sources, and interaction patterns. This data does not directly identify you as an individual.

Cookies and Similar Technologies

SnapCalc may use cookies and similar technologies to support core website functionality, measure usage patterns, and display advertising. Cookies are small text files stored on your device that allow the site to recognize repeat visits and understand how pages are used.

Third-party services integrated into this site, including analytics providers and advertising networks such as Google AdSense, may also place cookies or use similar identifiers. These technologies help deliver relevant advertisements, prevent fraud, and generate aggregate reporting on site usage.

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Be aware that disabling cookies may affect certain features or limit how effectively the site functions.

How Information Is Used

Information collected by SnapCalc is used strictly for legitimate and limited purposes related to operating and improving the website. Typical uses include:

  • Maintaining and improving calculator accuracy and usability
  • Understanding how visitors interact with site content
  • Diagnosing technical issues and monitoring site performance
  • Responding to messages, feedback, or support requests
  • Displaying advertising and affiliate content where applicable

No collected information is used for profiling individuals, automated decision-making, or purposes unrelated to the operation of the site.

Advertising and Affiliate Disclosure

SnapCalc may display advertising through third-party networks, including Google AdSense, as well as affiliate links to external products or services. These third parties may use cookies or similar technologies to measure ad performance and display ads that are relevant to users.

SnapCalc does not receive access to personal profiles created by advertisers, and any data collected by advertising partners is governed by their own privacy policies. Users are encouraged to review the privacy policies of those third parties for additional details.

Data Sharing and Third Parties

SnapCalc does not sell, rent, or trade personal information. Information may be shared only with service providers that support the operation of the site, such as hosting services, analytics platforms, and advertising networks, and only to the extent necessary for those services to function.

Information may also be disclosed if required by law, regulation, or legal process, or if necessary to protect the rights, safety, or integrity of the website and its users.

Data Retention

Personal information submitted through the contact form is retained only for as long as necessary to address the inquiry or maintain a reasonable communication record. Non-personal analytics data is retained according to the policies of the respective analytics or service providers.

International Use

SnapCalc is accessible to users worldwide. Depending on your location, information may be processed or stored in different countries where service providers operate. By using this site, you consent to such processing where permitted by applicable law.

Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your jurisdiction, including regions such as the European Union, the United Kingdom, and certain U.S. states, you may have rights related to personal data. These rights may include the ability to request access to information you have provided, request correction or deletion of that information, or object to certain forms of processing.

Requests related to data access or deletion can be submitted through the contact page. Reasonable steps may be taken to verify the request before fulfilling it.

Children’s Information

SnapCalc is not intended for children under the age of 13. The site does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information through this site, please contact us so that the information can be removed.

Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in legal requirements, site functionality, or business practices. Updates will be posted on this page, and continued use of the site after changes are made constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Information

If you have questions, concerns, or requests related to this Privacy Policy or data practices on SnapCalc, please use the contact page to get in touch. Privacy-related inquiries are reviewed and handled with care.
