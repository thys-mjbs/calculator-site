Assumptions and how to use this calculator

  • Use an average take-home month, not a best month.
  • Keep inputs limited to essentials, not discretionary spending.
  • If an input varies, estimate a realistic average across higher and lower months.
  • This tool does not assess shocks or long-term commitments.
  • Rerun after any meaningful change to income, housing, debt terms, or essential costs.
