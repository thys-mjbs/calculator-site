What this tool calculates

This paid tool computes total essentials, coverage ratio, and monthly margin. It then calculates the stable threshold gap and converts that gap into correction targets.

It also ranks the biggest expense levers and shows the required single-lever change needed to reach the stable threshold, capped to what that lever currently is.

Assumptions and limitations

  • All inputs are monthly amounts in the same unit.
  • Essentials are treated as must-pay obligations for baseline stability.
  • This tool does not model irregular bills, emergencies, or future commitments.
  • Lever targets are single-lever scenarios and may be unrealistic for small levers.
  • Results are only as accurate as the numbers you enter.
