Income vs Essentials Diagnostic (Pro Tool)

This paid tool stress-tests baseline readiness by applying reliability, variability, commitments, buffer targets, and estimation uncertainty. It does not model shocks or future commitments.

Your results

This output is a stress-tested view of baseline readiness. It applies income reliability and variability, adds commitments, increases essentials by an error margin, and then compares effective income to stressed essentials.

If the outcome is borderline or stable but confidence is low, treat the classification as fragile. Tighten estimates first before making new commitments.
