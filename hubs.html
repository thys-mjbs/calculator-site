Hubs

What hubs are for

Hubs are short, editorial entry points to major SnapCalc libraries. They are not just navigation. Each hub explains what the section is for, what kinds of tools you will find there, and what the outputs do and do not mean.

SnapCalc tools are designed to be practical and fast. Most results are estimates unless you have complete, verified inputs. If a decision is high-stakes, use outputs as a starting point and verify with source documents, constraints, and professional checks where applicable.

Calculators

The calculators hub is the main library. It contains focused, single-purpose tools built around real user questions, with minimal inputs and clear outputs. Each calculator runs in your browser and is designed to support quick checking, comparisons, and planning without forcing unnecessary complexity. Open the Calculators hub to browse the library.

For interpretation and assumptions, refer to Methodology and Accuracy.
