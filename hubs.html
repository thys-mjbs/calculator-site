Hubs

What hubs are for

Hubs are editorial entry points to major SnapCalc libraries. They are not simple navigation pages. Each hub explains what a section is for, what kinds of tools it contains, and how outputs should be interpreted.

SnapCalc tools are designed for speed and clarity. Results are often estimates unless complete, verified inputs are available. For high-stakes decisions, outputs should be used as a starting point and verified against source documents and constraints.

Calculators

The calculators hub contains focused, single-purpose tools built around real user questions. Each calculator runs locally in your browser and is designed to support quick checking, comparison, and planning. Visit the Calculators hub to browse the library.

For assumptions and interpretation guidance, see Methodology and Accuracy.
