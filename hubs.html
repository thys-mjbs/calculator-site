Hubs

What hubs are for

Hubs are simple entry points to the main SnapCalc libraries. They give you a quick explanation of what a section contains and what it is useful for, then take you straight into the tools.

Results depend on what you enter. Use outputs for quick checking and planning, and confirm anything important against your own figures and constraints.

If you want detail on how tools are designed and how to interpret results, read: About, Methodology, and Accuracy.

Calculators

The calculators library is the main collection. It contains focused, single-purpose tools that answer specific questions with minimal inputs and clear outputs. You can use it to sanity-check numbers, compare scenarios, and plan with basic assumptions.

Browse the library here: Calculators.
