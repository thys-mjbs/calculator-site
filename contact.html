Contact SnapCalc

This page is the fastest way to get in touch about anything on SnapCalc. If something looks wrong, feels confusing, or simply does not behave as expected, tell me. The site is actively maintained, and feedback is used to fix issues and improve clarity.

Open the contact form
Note: The form opens in a new tab.

Use the contact form for practical reports such as broken links, missing pages, incorrect calculations, formatting problems on mobile, or anything that causes a calculator to produce an unexpected result. If you can, include the exact page URL, the values you entered, and what you expected to happen. That makes it possible to reproduce the issue quickly and resolve it properly.

You can also use this form to request a new utility. If you searched the site and could not find a calculator for a specific task, describe what you were trying to work out and what inputs and outputs you would expect. If the request is clear and broadly useful, it will be added as a new tool or folded into an existing category where it fits naturally.

If you want to suggest an improvement to an existing calculator, be specific. Useful suggestions include adding an input that commonly matters, clarifying assumptions, improving error handling, or adjusting wording so the purpose is clearer. General opinions are fine, but actionable details are what lead to better tools.

The form opens in Google Forms and responses are handled by email. You will receive a reply when a follow-up is needed, when a fix is confirmed, or when a request has a clear next step. If your message is a simple acknowledgment or general praise without a question, a reply may not be necessary.
