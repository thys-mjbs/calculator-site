Tip calculator to work out tip amount and total bill

This tip calculator helps you calculate a tip from a bill amount and a tip percentage. It shows the tip amount and the total bill including tip, so you can decide what to pay without doing mental maths.

Enter the bill amount, enter the tip percentage you want to leave, then click Calculate. The result section will show the tip value and the final total you would pay.

This calculator is designed for simple tipping situations at restaurants, bars, delivery, and personal services where you want a fast answer and a clean total.

Assumptions and how to use this calculator

  • The bill amount is the amount you want to base the tip on (often the final bill including VAT).
  • The tip percentage applies to the bill amount you enter.
  • Results are rounded to two decimal places for typical card and cash payments.
  • This calculator does not split the bill across people. Use your bill split calculator for that.

Common questions

What tip percentage should I use?

It depends on local custom and service quality. Many people use 10 percent as a baseline, and 12.5 to 15 percent for good service. Use whatever feels appropriate for the situation.

Should I tip on the total bill including tax?

This calculator will tip on whatever bill amount you enter. If you prefer to tip on the pre-tax amount, enter the pre-tax value instead.

Can I use this for delivery or takeaway tips?

Yes. Enter the order total as the bill amount and choose your tip percentage. The calculator will show the tip and the final total.

Why do my totals look slightly different to my card charge?

Some payment terminals round differently, and some venues add a service charge. This tool rounds to two decimals and assumes no extra fees.
