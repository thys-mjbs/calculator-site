About this tip calculator

This tip calculator helps you work out how much extra to add to a bill and what your final total will be. Enter the bill amount, choose a tip percentage, and the tool shows you both the tip and the combined total in one line. It is designed for quick, practical use at restaurants, bars, taxis or any situation where you want to add a percentage on top of a base amount.

You can type whole numbers or decimals (for example 550.50). The input automatically formats larger numbers with commas so they are easier to read, while still calculating with the exact value in the background. The goal is simple: reduce mental arithmetic and make it obvious what you are actually paying.

Common questions

Do I have to use a specific currency?

No. The calculator is currency-agnostic. It only works with numbers. You decide which currency the amounts represent.

Can I use custom percentages?

Yes. Any percentage from 0 up to 1000 is allowed, so you can test normal tips, no tip, or extreme scenarios without limits.

Will this page change later?

Yes. The ad and affiliate areas are placeholders and will be replaced with live units once the site is fully configured. The core calculation behaviour will stay the same.
