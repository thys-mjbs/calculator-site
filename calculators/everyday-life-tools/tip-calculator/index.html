Tip calculator for tip amount and final bill total

This tip calculator helps you calculate the tip amount from a bill total and a tip percentage. It also shows the final total you would pay after adding the tip.

To use it, enter your bill amount, enter your tip percentage, then click Calculate. The results show the tip amount and the total including tip.

This is designed for simple tipping scenarios like restaurants, bars, delivery, salons, and any place where you want a fast, clear total without mental maths.

Assumptions and how to use this calculator

  • The bill amount is the amount you want to base the tip on (often the final bill including VAT).
  • The tip percentage applies to the bill amount you enter.
  • Results are rounded to two decimals for normal card or cash payments.
  • This calculator does not split the bill between people.

Common questions

What tip percentage should I use?

Many people use 10 percent as a baseline and 12.5 to 15 percent for good service. Local custom varies, so adjust as needed.

Can I tip on the pre tax amount instead?

Yes. Enter the pre tax amount as the bill amount if that is how you prefer to calculate your tip.

Why does my card terminal show a slightly different amount?

Some terminals round differently, and some venues include service charges. This tool assumes no extra fees and rounds to two decimals.

Is this the same as a service charge?

No. A service charge is an added fee set by the venue. A tip is optional and chosen by you. If a service charge is already included, you may want to tip less or not at all.
